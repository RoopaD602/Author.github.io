Biography

Chetan Bhagat is a famous novelist who writes in English and Hindi12.
He was born on 22 April 1974 in a Punjabifamily in New Delhi12345.
He studied in Army Public School, Dhaula Kuan (1978-1991)135,
then did his engineering degree from IIT Delhi (1991-1995)12345
and then his post-graduate degree from IIM Ahmedabad1234.
He worked as aninvestment banker in Hong Kong for nine years135,
where he developed his passion for writing5.

He wrote his first novel,
Five Point Someone, based on his hostel life at IIT Delhi,
which was published in 2004 by Rupa and Company15.
The novel was a huge success and made him a popular writer
a mong the youth5.

He quit his banking job and returned to India5.
He has written several other best-selling novels,some of which have been
adapted into Bollywood movies24. He alsowrites columns on youth
career development and current affairs for various newspapers4.
He has received many awards and recognition
including the Young Alumni Achiever Award 2018 from IIMA
in the arts and entertainment category4.
